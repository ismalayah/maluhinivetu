Who is Peyton Hillis?

Peyton Derek Hillis (born January 21, 1986) is a former American football running back who played in the NFL.

Hillis was a highly regarded recruit who attended Conway High School in Conway, Arkansas. Hillis studied at the University of Arkansas, where he mainly played fullback.

Peyton Hillis bio

Hillis moved up the depth chart to become the Denver Broncos’ starting fullback after being selected in the seventh round of the 2008 NFL Draft.

He was made the starter running back due to injuries to the other running backs. Hillis was dealt to the Cleveland Browns in 2010 after spending the previous two years in Denver. Hillis won a national poll in 2011 and was featured on the Madden NFL 12 video game cover by EA Sports.

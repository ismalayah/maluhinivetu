Irene Cara Cause of Death: The Singer Was Found Dead At Her Home

Irena Cara, 63, passed unexpectedly at her Florida home, according to a tweet from her publicist Judith Moose. What was Irena Cara’s cause of death?

Irene Cara passed away on Saturday. She was best remembered for her roles in the cult films Flashdance and Fame from the 1980s.

She stated, ‘It is with great regret that I announce the demise of Irene Cara on behalf of her family. The Oscar-winning actress, singer, songwriter, and producer died at home in Florida. Her cause of death is currently unknown, but we’ll let you know as we know more. The family of Irene has asked for privacy as they grieve.’ 

The statement added, ‘She was a wonderfully brilliant soul whose legacy will live forever via her music and films.’

The publicist said, ‘I’ll be reading each and every one of the comments and know she’ll be beaming from Heaven. Please share your thoughts and memories of the artist. She loved her followers.’

Cara, a 1959 Bronx, New York City native, first performed in theatre and television before winning the title part in the musical drama Sparkle in 1976.

Early in her life, Cara began working in the entertainment industry, including theatre, television, music, and movies.

The musician received her big break in 1980 when she was chosen to play Coco Hernandez in the renowned musical film Fame, which followed the trials and tribulations of a group of high school students in New York City.

Cara’s performance of the musical drama’s title song would go on to win an Academy Award for Best Original Song and garner the performer nods for two Grammy Awards, among other honors.

Irena Cara Obituary 

According to the publicist Moose, a tribute for Cara’s supporters will be held at a future date, and funeral arrangements are still being made.

The Family Respond To Rumors Behind The Cause Of Her Death

Irene Cara’s family released the following statement on her official Twitter account in reaction to viral rumors regarding the cause of her passing:

‘Thank you to everyone who took the time to share their sorrow. Please refrain from posting spam concerning cryptocurrency. Please refrain from blaming a vaccine for her passing, those who are doing so. Until an autopsy is finished, we won’t have any medical answers.’

Tributes were paid to her on Social Media.

On Saturday, condolences for Cara flooded social media.

‘More than you would ever know, Irene Cara, you inspired me. Your singing and songwriting generated unstoppable intensity,’ Lenny Kravitz, a musician, stated on Twitter.

Who is Lil Twin:

Lil Twin is a famous Rapper. He was born on April 17, 1997 and his birthplace is Dayton, OH. Lil is also well known as, Southern rapper who found fame as one half of the duo Team Twin alongside his twin brother Twin Twin. He and his brother have built a fanbase through YouTube with videos like “Chiraq,” “Million” and “Have My Money.”

Lil is originated from United States. He and his brother uploaded a tribute to Michael Jackson’s “Thriller” video on their YouTube channel.

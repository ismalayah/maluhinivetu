Pop Smoke or Bashar Barakah Jackson was an American who was a popular singer, rapper, and songwriter by profession. He is mainly known for his breakout single titled Welcome to the Party in the year 2019.

He was also known for his popular mixtapes like Meet the Woo and Meet the Woo 2. On 03 July 2020, his album titled Shoot for the Stars, Aim for the Moon was released, to pay a tribute to him after four months of his death.

Pop Smoke Wiki/Biography

Born on 20 July 1999, Pop Smoke’s age was 20 years when he took his last breathe on 19 February 2020. He was brought up and raised in an upper-middle-class Christian family from Canarsie, Brooklyn, New York, United States. He was known to be an American by nationality and belongs to the Christianity religion. He completed his primary schooling at nine different schools in Brooklyn, New York.

He was suspended from his school for bringing a gun in school when he was just in the eighth class. After that, he spends two years on house arrest as a punishment for his deed. After that, he moved to Philadelphia and enrolled himself at Rocktop Academy, but later on, he was diagnosed with a heart murmur and forced to leave school. He started playing basketball and turned to street life.

Career

In the year 2019, he was signed by Victor Victor Worldwide of Universal Music and after some time he released his debut breakout single Welcome to the Party. Later on, two remixes of this song were also released starring Nicki Minaj and Skepta.

Controversies

Pop Smoke was landed into a controversy on 17 January 2020, when he was arrested by the authorities at the International Airport when he was returning from a tour. As per the reports, he was accused of stealing a Rolls Royce worth $375,000 from a person in California when he borrowed the car for one day but didn’t return on time.

The car was found from his mother’s home in the Canarsie section of Brooklyn after few days after his arrest. The police officials also tried to question him about Crips, but he refused to answer. He had to post a bond of $250,000 as a punishment and had to agree on the fact that he would stay away from known gang members of Crips.

Death

Pop Smoke took his last breathe on 19 February 2020, when he was shot dead by four hooded men inside the house in Hollywood Hills, California. As per the official statements, four men broke into his house and shot him twice in the chest. After the incident, he was taken to the nearby Medical Center where he was declared dead after few hours of inspections from doctors.

His remains were buried at Green-Wood Cemetery. After few days three adult men and two minors were arrested concerning his murder. As per official statements, Pop Smoke’s death was related to his connection to a drug-smuggling-based gang named Crips.

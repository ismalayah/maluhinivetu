How tall is MrBeast?

MrBeast, a popular YouTuber known for his philanthropic acts and grandiose challenges, remains one of the platform’s top earners after nearly a decade of making content. Despite the name recognition he has earned over the years, a few questions remain about this prominent YouTuber and philanthropist. Namely, people really want to know his height.

Recommended Videos

Maybe it’s due to the numerous videos and pictures floating around the web in which MrBeast — whose real name is Jimmy Donaldson — can be seen towering over his companions.

MrBeast’s height

A few photos indicate that the veteran YouTuber stands above 6 feet, sparking an insatiable desire from fans to discover his true height.

Consensus seems to agree that Donaldson stands at approximately 6 ft 3 in tall. That places him several inches, at least, above many of his fellow YouTubers, and even some larger-than-life stars, like Chris Evans.

Photos on Instagram reveal MrBeast truly does tower over others at his high stature. He’s tall enough to touch a stop sign with ease, stands high over cars, and is regularly front-and-center in group photos due to the extra inches he has on his colleagues.

MrBeast’s height is far less important than his philanthropic efforts, of course — which, on top of planting trees and digging wells, include projects like giving thousands of Christmas presents to children and opening Beast Philanthropy, a new charity — but his constantly-increasing popularity is bound to spark plenty more questions about the man behind the kind deeds.

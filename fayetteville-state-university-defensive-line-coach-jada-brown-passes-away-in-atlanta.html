Fayetteville State University Defensive Line Coach Jada Brown passes away in Atlanta

Fayetteville, NC – The Fayetteville State University community is in mourning following the sudden and tragic passing of Defensive Line Coach Jada Brown from Atlanta, Georgia. The news of his death has left family, friends, and colleagues in shock and disbelief.

Confirmation of Coach Brown’s passing came through a heartfelt Facebook post expressing the profound loss of a vibrant and young life. The post stated, “The amount of male friends and family members I have lost is truly heartbreaking so young so full of life. Yesterday we lost another one. Hard to even process. Love you forever like a brother Jada Brown.”

Shadora Danmola shared a post on fb on the death of Jada:

Jada Brown I remember the last time we talked. It’s was like yesterday. Gone to soon. Going to be missed . R.I.P

The cause of Jada Brown’s untimely death has not been disclosed at this time, adding to the somber atmosphere surrounding the news.

Jada Brown’s educational journey began at The University of Memphis, where he pursued his undergraduate studies, graduating with a Bachelor of Arts in Communication in 2010. His dedication to collegiate sports coaching commenced at Hampden-Sydney College in Farmville, VA, where he served as the Defensive Line/Strength and Conditioning coach.

Over the years, Coach Brown contributed his expertise to various institutions, including Clark Atlanta University, Reinhardt University, the University of Tennessee, and the University of Florida. His coaching roles involved not only defensive line coaching but also encompassed responsibilities such as Video Coordinator/Tight-End at Reinhardt University and Prospect Camp Coordinator at the University of Florida.

Continuing his coaching journey, Coach Brown served at Mansfield University of Pennsylvania as the Defensive Line/Long Snapper Coach before joining Methodist University as the Defensive Line coach from March 2019 to July 2021. In July 2021, he took on the position of Defensive Line coach at Fayetteville State University.

Coach Jada Brown’s impact on the collegiate sports community was significant, and his untimely passing has left a void that will be deeply felt. The university and the broader sports community extend their condolences to Coach Brown’s family and loved ones during this difficult time.

As details surrounding the cause of death remain undisclosed, the community awaits further information, remembering Coach Jada Brown for his dedication and passion for the game.

Ty Murray Biography

Ty Murray was born on 11 October 1969, in Phoenix, AZ. American bull rider; World Champion nine times rodeo. He guest-starred with Chuck Norris on an episode of Walker, Texas Ranger. He was an All-Around Champion of the National High School Rodeo Association.

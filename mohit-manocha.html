Career

Mohit Manocha started his YouTube career in 2018 with his first video named “Cheap International Travel – An Indian Backpacker Explains – Cheap Flights, Hotels, Visas and more.“ He posts videos related to budget-friendly traveling around the world. Mohit has traveled to many countries across the world including the United States, Canada, and more than 20 countries in Europe. Mohit has gained more than 1.3 Million subscribers to his YouTube account in a time span of just 2 years. Later, He also starting posting daily vlogs on his channel.
